B.Sc in Animation

Eligibility: 10+2 passed / Equivalent

The B.Sc. (H) Animation and Multimedia course at Swami Vivekananda University, which is among the best B.Sc. multimedia and animation colleges in Kolkata, is designed to cater to the growing demand for skilled animators and multimedia professionals. The B.Sc. in Animation and Multimedia programme equips students with the necessary artistic and technical skills required to excel in the field of animation and multimedia. The curriculum of the B.Sc. Animation & Multimedia course takes a multidisciplinary approach, combining aspects of fine arts, storytelling, graphic design, and technology.

B.Sc. in Animation & Multimedia Course Details: Take a 4-year ride where technology and creativity come together! Our B.Sc. in Animation course gives you industry-ready skills in 2D/3D animation, VFX, and digital storytelling. Available to enthusiastic students who have cleared 10+2 or equivalent, this course brings imagination to life in the form of breathtaking visual masterpieces!

Programme Overview

Students receive hands-on training in various animation techniques, such as 2D, 3D, and stop-motion animation

  • The course offers exposure to industry-standard software and tools such as Adobe Creative Suite, Maya, and Blender.
  • Students engage in practical projects that encourage creativity, problem-solving, and critical thinking.
  • The university organizes workshops, guest lectures, and industry interactions with leading professionals to expose students to real-world scenarios and current trends.
  • Faculty members are acclaimed artists, animators, and industry experts who provide mentorship and guidance to students, fostering their artistic skills and technical knowledge.
